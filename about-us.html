About Us

Wall of Thoughts is a student-led mental health awareness project, dedicated to providing an anonymous safe space to confront, share, and understand the thoughts we often hide.

Our Mission

To break the silence around mental struggles, empower self-expression, and remind everyone that facing your thoughts is a step toward healing.
